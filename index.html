A bit about me

Studied at a BSC in Computer Science at Newcastle University, completing my degree working with Nissan on my dissertation.

Currently working as a Junior Front End Developer at Qrious. Responsible for developing high standards of code which meet our high internal standards.

Proficient in HTML, CSS (SCSS and Less), JavaScript and with some experience in C# (.Net Core), PHP and MySQL. Also experienced with libraries such as jQuery and Bootstrap.

I have worked with a variety of clients such as Nord Anglia Education, Gosforth Group, North East Learning Trust, Dementia Pathways, UTC South Durham, Bookfit and GymSync.

Outside of work I spend time developing my own projects to not only improve my knowledge of web development, but to also learn new skills that will help my career.

Constantly trying to find new ways to expand my knowledge of web development and am always eager to take on new challenges.
